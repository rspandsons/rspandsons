About Us

Who we are

Our company is a leading provider of construction project services and solutions, with a strong focus on the Oil & Gas sector. Our founder, Brijesh Kumar Pachauri, has over 30 years of experience in this industry, and has worked on a wide range of projects, from small-scale installations to large-scale refineries.

At our company, we take great pride in our ability to provide comprehensive solutions to our clients, and we have a proven track record of delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. We offer a range of services to our clients, including piping, structural, and storage tank installation, as well as equipment erection, copper work, and shot blasting and painting.

Who we are

Our company is a leading provider of construction project services and solutions, with a strong focus on the Oil & Gas sector. Our founder, Brijesh Kumar Pachauri, has over 30 years of experience in this industry, and has worked on a wide range of projects, from small-scale installations to large-scale refineries.

At our company, we take great pride in our ability to provide comprehensive solutions to our clients, and we have a proven track record of delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards. We offer a range of services to our clients, including piping, structural, and storage tank installation, as well as equipment erection, copper work, and shot blasting and painting.

We have rendered our service in the construction of several Refineries, Petrochemicals and Storage terminals in the country under the inspection of reputed agencies like, ENGINEERS INDIA LTD., TOYO ENGINEERING, ABB LUMMUS CREST, TATA CONSULTING ENGINEERS, INDIAN OIL CORPORATION LIMITED, HINDUSTAN PETROLEUM LTD, RELIANCE PETROLEUM LTD and more.

Brijesh Kumar Pachauri

Our company is owned by a highly skilled and experienced professional with over 30 years of core experience in the Oil & Gas sector and Construction Project services. Throughout his career, he has gained invaluable knowledge and expertise in various areas, including copper blasting, structural erection, and mechanical installation. His passion for excellence and commitment to quality are the driving force behind our company's success.
He ensures that our team of professionals are trained and equipped with the latest technology and techniques to deliver high-quality services to our clients. His extensive industry knowledge allows him to understand the unique challenges that arise in different projects and to develop effective solutions to overcome them. Overall, his years of experience and expertise in the industry are a testament to our company's dedication to delivering exceptional service and exceeding our clients' expectations.
